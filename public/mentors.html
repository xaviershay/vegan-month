Melbourne Mentors

Xavier Shay

Xavier lives in a repurposed dairy in East Melbourne, which is somewhat fitting for a vegan. He has extensive international teaching experience in fields from dance to programming that he brings to this mentoring role. He is big into rock climbing, playing the piano, and makes a mean burrito.

Jared Shay

Jared is primarily a drummer, playing in bands of all different genres and teaching at a number of schools. He's handy in the kitchen, as evidenced by his abandoned-yet-timeless cooking blog. More up to date, he keeps a personal development blog TwoShay with Xavier and juggles better than most people you know. Mention Star Wars to him and you'll have a friend for life.

Jodie Foreman

Jodie's profession is stilt-walker and creator/performer of large scale puppets, with some swing dancing, rock-climbing and running on the side. She has an associate diploma in natural therapies and a love of cooking and eating in restaurants. Jodie was drawn to veganism in equal parts by ethics and health.
